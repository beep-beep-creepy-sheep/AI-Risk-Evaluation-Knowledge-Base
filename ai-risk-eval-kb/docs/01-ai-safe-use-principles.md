# 01. AI Safe-Use Principles / AI 安全使用原则

## Purpose / 目的

These principles help analysts use AI tools responsibly for learning, drafting, summarizing, and evaluation design. They are not a substitute for compliance review, human judgment, or approved investment processes.

## Confidentiality / 保密性

Never provide AI tools with confidential information. This includes client records, internal memos, non-public research, account numbers, employee details, system credentials, unpublished performance data, or proprietary portfolio construction logic.

## No Client Data / 不使用客户数据

Do not paste client names, client portfolios, account identifiers, transaction histories, addresses, emails, phone numbers, or relationship notes. Use mock examples that are clearly fictional.

## No Confidential Internal Content / 不使用内部保密内容

Avoid internal documents, meeting notes, unpublished investment views, private models, non-public risk dashboards, or real operational incidents. If a workflow requires internal information, handle it through approved internal systems and human review.

## Human Review / 人工复核

AI output should be treated as a draft. A qualified person should verify facts, sources, formulas, assumptions, limitations, and suitability before any professional use.

## Source Checking / 来源核验

Ask models to distinguish between provided context and general knowledge. Require citations or source references when using public research. Verify important claims against original public sources.

## Numerical Validation / 数值验证

Portfolio metrics, Excel formulas, performance calculations, and risk statistics must be checked independently. AI systems can misread units, time periods, formulas, and assumptions.

## Auditability / 可审计性

Keep records of prompts, model outputs, review notes, eval results, prompt versions, and known limitations. This supports repeatable improvement and clear accountability.
