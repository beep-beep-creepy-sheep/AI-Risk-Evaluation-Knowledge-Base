# 03. AI Risk Taxonomy / AI 风险分类

## Hallucination

The model invents facts, sources, calculations, or details not supported by the provided context.

## Data Leakage

The model exposes sensitive information from prompts, files, logs, or unintended context.

## Prompt Injection

The model follows malicious or irrelevant instructions embedded in user-provided content instead of the system or task instructions.

## Numerical Inaccuracy

The model produces incorrect formulas, calculations, units, dates, assumptions, or metric interpretations.

## Overreliance

Users treat model output as authoritative without human review, independent validation, or source checking.

## Unsupported Investment Recommendation

The model gives personalized, definitive, or unapproved buy, sell, hold, allocation, or timing advice.

## Source Reliability

The model uses weak, missing, misquoted, outdated, or unverifiable sources.

## Confidentiality

The model requests, stores, reveals, or normalizes use of confidential or internal information.

## Bias

The model produces unfair, unsupported, or systematically skewed analysis based on inappropriate assumptions.

## Record-Keeping and Auditability

The workflow lacks prompt versions, test records, source references, reviewer notes, or change history.
